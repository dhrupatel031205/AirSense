Partnership with NASA

EcoSky is built on the foundation of NASA's TEMPO satellite mission, bringing space-grade air quality monitoring to everyone.

NASA TEMPO Mission

Tropospheric Emissions: Monitoring of Pollution

TEMPO is the first satellite instrument to monitor air quality over North America hourly during daytime. Launched in 2023, it provides unprecedented detail about air pollution from space.
